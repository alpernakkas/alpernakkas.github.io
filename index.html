The University of Texas at Arlington · College of Business

I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Information Systems and Operations Management at The University of Texas at Arlington. My research focuses on retail operations, supply chain management, and behavioral operations management, with related interests in game theory, network theory, category management, retail collaboration practices, and revenue management.

Areas of Focus

Research Interests

· · ·
🛒
Retail Operations & Category Management

Research on retail assortment planning, category captainship, planogram design, display fees, and manufacturer–retailer coordination in competitive retail settings.

🚚
Supply Chain Management

Analytical work on operational decision allocation, supply chain networks, pricing, incentives, and coordination problems across upstream and downstream firms.

🎯
Behavioral Operations & Game Theory

Theory-driven research on strategic behavior, bargaining, network interactions, and decision-making frictions in operations and supply chain environments.
