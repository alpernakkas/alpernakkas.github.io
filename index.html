Operations Management Supply Chain Management Retail Operations Category Management Behavioral Operations Network Theory

I am an Associate Professor at The University of Texas at Arlington. My research focuses on retail operations, supply chain management, and behavioral operations management. I study manufacturer–retailer interactions, category captainship, assortment and planogram decisions, supply chain networks, and related strategic questions in operations and management science.

Supply Chain Management

Behavioral Operations Management

How strategic behavior, bargaining, and decision frictions shape performance in operations and supply chain contexts.

Network Theory in Operations

Analytical work on how network position, valuation heterogeneity, and structure affect equilibrium outcomes.
